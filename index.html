Our story

Through Guru Nanak’s concept of the community kitchen, while staying and growing together at the Karta Purakh Niwas, a bunch of us always knew we wanted to work together on something impactful.

Serving free food at various locations every morning, we would often wonder how amazing it would be if we could contribute in providing hygienic and healthy food to the larger population.

One of us had already been running a small biscuit business from his home for some time and the response from customers was far exceeding the expectations. KPN Foods was born when his small venture was ordained to scale!

Baking at KPN

Bringing healthy food choices to one and all!

A standard day at KPN Bakery starts with Meditation sessions and learning to do Sewa. This helps workers to keep their spirits high throughout the day. Then the plan of the entire day is worked out. All the utensils are sterilized and inspected for hygiene. Preparations are done with the most healthy ingredients available in the market. The taste test is always the best part of the day 🧡
